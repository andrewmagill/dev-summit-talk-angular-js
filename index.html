The Bad

The Ugly

Directives - The Story So Far

  • Directive bind data in $scope to DOM elements
  • There are lots of built-in directives like ng-repeat, ng-filter and ng-link
  • What if we could bind data in $scope to other things? Charts, Maps ect...
